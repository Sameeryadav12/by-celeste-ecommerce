# Sprint 4 Retrospective

## What Went Well

During Sprint 4, the team completed important features such as Product Management, Order Management, Admin Login, and UI/UX improvements. Communication between team members improved compared to Sprint 3, and regular meetings helped everyone stay updated.

The client was happy with the progress and mentioned that the website looked much more complete than before.

---

## What Did Not Go Well
Effective team communication

Some tasks such as Checkout Testing and Bug Fixing were not fully completed during this sprint. Testing mostly happened towards the end, which created some pressure for the team.

There were also small delays when frontend work depended on backend APIs being completed first.


---

## What Can Be Improved

For the next sprint, testing should start earlier so issues can be identified sooner.

Large tasks should also be divided into smaller subtasks to make progress tracking easier.
