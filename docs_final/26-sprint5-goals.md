# Sprint 5 Planning – Final Release, Wholesale Enhancements and Handover

## Sprint 5 Goal

To complete the final release of the By Celeste website by carrying forward unfinished Sprint 4 work, finalising wholesale and advanced features, improving mobile usability, SEO and performance, completing system testing, collecting client feedback, and preparing the final handover package.

## Sprint Context

Sprint 5 is the final sprint for the By Celeste e-commerce website project. The team reviewed the original project proposal, Sprint 3 deliverables, Sprint 4 outcomes, and remaining client requirements before finalising the Sprint 5 backlog.

The sprint includes three main types of work:

- Sprint 4 carry-forward work
- Wholesale and advanced feature finalisation
- Final testing, client review, and handover preparation


## Carry-Forward Work

The following work was carried forward from previous sprint

- Replace demo products with real By Celeste product data
- Complete checkout testing and fixes
- Complete final bug fixing
- Improve system stability before final handover

These items were prioritised first because they directly affect the reliability, quality, and client-readiness of the final website.
