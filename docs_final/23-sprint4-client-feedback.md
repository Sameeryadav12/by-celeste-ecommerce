# Sprint 4 Client Feedback

At the end of Sprint 4, the team conducted a sprint review meeting and presented the completed work to the client.

The client was very happy with the progress made during this sprint. Compared to Sprint 3, she mentioned that the website looked much more complete and closer to a real working system.

The client was especially happy with:
- Product Management features
- Admin Login system
- UI/UX improvements
- Brand trust messaging

During the meeting, the client requested that the brand trust messaging should appear slightly bigger so it is easier for customers to notice. The team updated this change immediately.

The client also informed the team that she is currently updating the final product information and will provide the updated data later.

Overall, the feedback from the client was positive, and the team will continue improving the system during Sprint 5.
